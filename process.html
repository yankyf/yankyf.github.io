Common Questions

Everything You Need
to Know

How do I send my photos?+

You can upload photos through our upload link, email a download link from your photographer (with password if needed), or drop off a USB or CD at our studio.

How long does the process take?+

Design typically takes 3–5 business days. After your approval, printing and delivery take 7–14 business days. Rush orders available upon request.

How many photos can I include?+

Full design albums include an unlimited number of photos blended across spreads. Non-design albums follow a standard one or two photos per page layout.

What sizes and covers are available?+

We offer square (8×8, 10×10, 12×12), portrait, and landscape sizes. Cover options include leather, acrylic, linen, wood, glass, metal, and more — with custom stamping and gilding available.

Can I request revisions?+

Absolutely. We send a full digital proof before printing and work with you until every page feels perfect. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Do you ship?+

Yes — we ship anywhere. Albums are carefully packaged to arrive in perfect condition. Local pickup is also available.
